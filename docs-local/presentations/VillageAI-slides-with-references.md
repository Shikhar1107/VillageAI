## How to Handle Questions on Data

### Common Questions & Responses:

**Q: "Where did you get 146 million farmers?"**
A: "From the Agriculture Census 2015-16, conducted by the Department of Agriculture. It's the most authoritative source, though some estimates vary between 120-146 million depending on the definition used."

**Q: "Is 30% crop loss realistic?"**
A: "Yes, ICAR and FAO studies show 15-25% losses from pests/diseases alone, plus 10-15% from weather and poor practices. These are preventable losses with timely advice."

**Q: "How confident are you in 20% income increase?"**
A: "This is based on documented impact from similar services - Digital Green showed 23%, Reuters Market Light 16-20%. We're being conservative with 20%."

**Q: "Is ₹13 per query sustainable?"**
A: "Yes, this includes ₹9 for a 3-minute call at bulk rates, ₹2 for cloud processing, and ₹2 for 10% human escalation. At scale, this can reduce to ₹8-10."

**Q: "Why only 1 million farmers in 5 years?"**
A: "That's less than 1% of total farmers - very conservative. mKisan reached 1.5 million, and they're text-only. With voice and dialects, we could exceed this."
