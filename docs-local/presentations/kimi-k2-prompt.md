# Kimi-K2 Prompt for VillageAI Presentation Generation

## Task
Create a professional, visually compelling PowerPoint presentation for VillageAI, a voice-first AI agricultural assistant for rural Indian farmers. The presentation should be suitable for hackathons, investor pitches, and government demonstrations.

## Presentation Requirements

### Overall Style
- **Design Theme**: Modern, clean, with agricultural and technology elements
- **Color Palette**: Green (#2E7D32), Saffron (#FF9800), Blue (#1976D2), White
- **Fonts**: Montserrat for headers, Open Sans for body, Noto Sans for Hindi/Tamil
- **Slide Count**: 20 slides + backup slides
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Language**: English with Hindi phrases where impactful

### Visual Generation Requirements

For each slide, generate or source appropriate visuals:

1. **Title Slide**:
   - Generate: Sunrise over Indian farmland panorama
   - Add: Phone icon merged with wheat stalks as logo
   - Include: Hindi tagline in Devanagari script

2. **Problem Slide**:
   - Generate: Split infographic showing struggling farmer
   - Create: India map with language diversity heat zones
   - Design: Icon set showing barriers (no internet, no smartphone)

3. **Solution Slide**:
   - Generate: Happy farmer using basic phone in field
   - Create: 3-step circular process diagram (missed call → callback → solution)
   - Design: Language bubble cloud showing 15+ dialects

4. **Technical Architecture**:
   - Create: Clean technical flow diagram with icons
   - Generate: Layered architecture visualization
   - Show: Progressive enhancement from MVP to Scale

5. **Impact Metrics**:
   - Design: Dashboard-style metrics display
   - Create: Before/after comparison images
   - Generate: Growth charts and graphs

6. **Success Stories**:
   - Generate: Farmer portrait illustrations (diverse demographics)
   - Create: Income improvement visualization
   - Design: Testimonial cards with regional authenticity

### Specific Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts for AI image generation:

1. **Hero Image**: "Indian farmer in traditional dress standing in green wheat field, holding basic Nokia-style feature phone to ear, smiling, sunrise in background, photorealistic style"

2. **Problem Visualization**: "Split screen showing: left side - confused farmer looking at diseased tomato plants, right side - long queue of farmers waiting outside government agriculture office, illustration style"

6. **Impact Visual**: "Before and after comparison: wilted cotton plants vs healthy cotton field, with percentage improvement overlay, photorealistic"

7. **Partnership Ecosystem**: "Circular ecosystem diagram with VillageAI at center, surrounded by government buildings, telecom towers, university, NGO symbols, flat icon style"

8. **Farmer Diversity**: "Collage of 6 Indian farmers - young woman in saree, elderly man in turban, middle-aged man in dhoti, young man in shirt, woman in salwar, man in lungi - all with phones, illustration style"

### Data Visualization Requirements

Create these charts/graphs:

1. **Adoption Curve**: Line graph showing exponential growth from 1,500 to 1 million farmers over 5 years
2. **Cost Analysis**: Bar chart showing cost reduction from ₹11,867 to ₹500 per farmer
3. **Language Coverage**: Donut chart showing dialect distribution
4. **Query Categories**: Horizontal bar chart - Disease (35%), Weather (25%), Prices (20%), Others (20%)
5. **Revenue Streams**: Pie chart with 5 segments as specified
6. **Impact Metrics**: KPI cards showing 20% income increase, 30% crop loss reduction
7. **ROI Timeline**: Area chart showing path to profitability by Year 2

### Animation Instructions

Add subtle animations:
- Slide transitions: Fade or Push
- Text: Appear on click for bullet points
- Diagrams: Build sequentially
- Charts: Animate data drawing
- Images: Subtle zoom or pan
- Icons: Gentle pulse for emphasis

### Content Hierarchy

Each slide should follow:
1. **Clear headline** (7-10 words max)
2. **3-5 bullet points** or single powerful visual
3. **Supporting imagery** (50% of slide space)
4. **Data/proof points** where relevant
5. **Minimal text**, maximum visual impact

### Special Slides to Emphasize

**Must be visually stunning:**
- Slide 4: Solution overview (this is the "aha" moment)
- Slide 11: Impact stories (emotional connection)
- Slide 13: Partnership ecosystem (credibility)
- Slide 16: Growth roadmap (vision)

### Language Integration

Include Hindi text strategically:
- Slide 1: "कृषि मित्र" (Krishi Mitra)
- Slide 4: "घर बैठे, अपनी भाषा में, मुफ्त कृषि सलाह"
- Slide 20: "हर किसान तक पहुंच, हर खेत तक सलाह"

Use regional language samples in voice/dialect slides.

### Icon Requirements

Create/source consistent icon set:
- Feature phone (not smartphone)
- Voice waves/audio
- Crops: wheat, rice, cotton, tomato
- Weather: sun, rain, cloud
- Rupee symbol
- Shield (safety)
- Clock (24/7)
- Globe with India
- People/farmers
- AI brain/chip

### Background Patterns

Use subtle backgrounds:
- Slides 1-3: Farm field texture (very light)
- Slides 4-8: Tech circuit pattern (barely visible)
- Slides 9-12: Geographic/map elements
- Slides 13-16: Network/connection pattern
- Slides 17-20: Combination of farm and tech

### Call-to-Action Slides

Make CTAs prominent:
- QR codes for more information
- Demo phone number in large, clear text
- Website/contact clearly visible
- Social media handles
- "Schedule Demo" button design

### Backup Slide Themes

Prepare additional slides for Q&A:
1. Detailed technical architecture
2. Full financial projections
3. Competitive analysis matrix
4. Research methodology
5. Team credentials
6. Awards and recognition
7. Media coverage
8. Detailed pilot results

## Output Format

Generate:
1. PowerPoint file (.pptx) with all slides
2. PDF version for easy sharing
3. Individual slide images (PNG) for social media
4. Executive summary document (2-pager)
5. Speaker notes for each slide

## Quality Checks

Ensure:
- All text is readable (minimum 18pt font)
- Images are high resolution (1920x1080 minimum)
- Color contrast meets accessibility standards
- No more than 6 lines of text per slide
- Consistent formatting throughout
- All data is accurate from source documents
- Hindi/Tamil text is properly rendered
- Charts are clearly labeled
- No copyright-protected images used

## Final Notes

This presentation should:
- Tell a compelling story (problem → solution → impact)
- Balance emotion and data
- Be understandable to diverse audiences
- Showcase technical competence without overwhelming
- Inspire action and support
- Be memorable and shareable

Remember: The goal is to show how VillageAI transforms the lives of rural Indian farmers through accessible, voice-first AI technology that works on any phone, in any language, without internet.
